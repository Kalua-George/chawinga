Background

Temwa was born on 20 September 1998, in Rumphi district, Northern Region, Malawi.
She is the youngest of five children. She is a Tumbuka by tribe and her name Temwa
means "love" in Tumbuka language. Her older sister, Tabitha Chawinga, is also
a professional

On January 3, 2024, Chawinga signed a two-year contract with the Kansas City Current.
